Sponsor Helvetic Ruby

After a successful 2023, Helvetic Ruby is back in 2024. We want to bring together professionals, experts, juniors and career changers to network and learn together. We’re dedicated to building an excellent line up of speakers and hosting a great event for the Swiss Ruby community.

Event facts

  • Conference for up to 200 attendees
  • We encourage underrepresented groups and career changers to join the conference through ticket sponsorships
  • Speakers from the Swiss and international tech community
  • Opportunity for networking with the Ruby and Rails tech community
  • Social media coverage

How we plan to use your contribution, mainly:

  • Event space and catering
  • Sponsor the travel and accommodation of speakers
  • Tickets sponsorships for underrepresented groups and career starters
  • Video recordings and photos
